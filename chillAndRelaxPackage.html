Relax & experience Maldivian life at B.Maalhos

All inclusive stay for 8 days, 7 nights

Nestled amidst the azure waters of the Maldives, B. Maalhos beckons travelers to indulge in a slice of paradise. Here, pristine beaches stretch as far as the eye can see, offering a haven of tranquility for those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. Sink your toes into the soft, powdery sands and feel the warmth of the sun on your skin as gentle ocean breezes whisper through palm trees, creating an atmosphere of serenity and bliss...

Experience authentic Maldivian culture and warm hospitality on the shores of B. Maalhos. Immerse yourself in local communities, savoring delicious cuisine and engaging in traditional activities. Whether you're exploring underwater wonders or relaxing on pristine beaches, B. Maalhos offers unforgettable moments in paradise. Come discover the beauty and charm of this island gem, where every experience leaves a lasting impression.

Package highlights

Highlights

Hanifaru Bay visit
Visit to a UNESCO biosphere reserve with bustling marine life, with a good chance of the opportunity to swim with manta rays and whale sharks..

Local island tour
Explore another inhabited island and get to know better the life of local islanders..

Sunset fishing
Experience hand-line fishing as the sun sets over the horizon..

Hibalhidhoo desert island trip
Explore a pristine desert island, and enjoy its lush vegetation, sandy beaches, and turquoise waters..

Luxurious 4 or 5-star Guesthouse Stay
The package features accommodation at a prestigious 4 or 5-star guesthouse in Rasdhoo, ensuring participants experience the utmost in comfort and hospitality while being conveniently located near the dive sites. This luxurious guesthouse stay adds an extra layer of indulgence to the overall diving adventure in the Maldives.

Optional Excursions

Contact us to include add ons.

Morning big game fishing
If you enjoy fishing or would like to try something new, then this could be for you. The vessel is fully equipped with rods and lures, and sets sail in the morning. Jackfish, barracuda, wahoo and yellow-fin tuna are just some of the fish you may be able to catch in the deeper waters.

Aidhoo farm island trip
Located near Hanifaru Bay, Aidhoo is home to lush greenery and ivory beaches. Bring your family and friends to explore and learn about the organic plantation process carried out on this coral island. Complimentary drinks, snacks & water will be provided..

Day 1: Arrival in Maldives
- Arrival at Velana International Airport.
- Travel to B. Maalhos via Dharanvandhoo.

Day 2-7: All excursions*
Hibalhidhoo desert island trip
Take a trip to your very own uninhabited island, where you can soak up the sun or relax in the shade of a palm tree. The turquoise-colored waters are all yours as well, so that you can enjoy swimming and, if you bring along your snorkeling equipment, take time to explore the fascinating underwater world.

Local island tour
An excursion for those who would like to experience a taste of all that the Maldives has to offer from different islands. Listen to the locals, sit and chatter about the day’s fishing in the shade of the trees, relax in ‘jolies’, the woven chairs.

Swim with mantas at Hanifaru Bay
Hanifaru Bay, a UNESCO biosphere reserve, is one of the few places in the world where mantas and whale sharks aggregate in unusually high numbers, especially between May and November. Snorkeling with these peaceful ocean giants is an unmissable experience.

Sunset fishing
Reef bottom fishing is a traditional activity in the Maldives, which guests can try for themselves as the sun sets over the horizon. Potential catches such as humpback snapper and groupers can also be cooked by our chefs..

Day 8: Departure
- Travel to Velana International Airport.
- Departure from Maldives.

*Note:The excursions and activities will be carried out during the period. Specific days will be selected based on the weather and other conditions to maximize your chances of having the best experience.

Speedboat Transfer
Enjoy a scenic speedboat transfer from the main international airport in Malé to Rasdhoo, providing a comfortable and direct journey across the turquoise waters of the Maldives.

Domestic Flight and Speedboat Combo
Opt for a domestic flight to the nearby Dharavandhoo Airport, followed by a short speedboat ride to Rasdhoo. This option offers a swift and efficient means of reaching the island.

Seaplane Transfer
For a more picturesque arrival, choose a seaplane transfer to Rasdhoo, allowing you to take in stunning aerial views of the Maldivian atolls before landing in the crystal-clear lagoon near Rasdhoo.

Airport Meet and Greet
Upon arrival at the main international airport in Malé, guests will be warmly welcomed by our representatives stationed at the arrival area. They will assist with luggage and guide guests to the designated transportation.

Speedboat or Seaplane Transfer Coordination
Our staff will coordinate the transfer logistics, ensuring that guests seamlessly board the speedboat, domestic flight, or seaplane, depending on the chosen mode of transportation. Clear communication and assistance will be provided throughout the transfer process. Arrival at Rasdhoo
Upon reaching Rasdhoo, guests will be met by our representatives at the island's arrival point, where they will assist with luggage and facilitate the check-in process at the selected 4 or 5-star guesthouse, ensuring a smooth and welcoming start to their stay.
