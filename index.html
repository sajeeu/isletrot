A little about us...

Isletrot Travel and Tours is the first travel agency in the Maldives focusing on the Maldivian experience, with solutions curated to enable you to make the most out of what the Maldives has to offer. Our unique approach diverges with the geographical and establishment-level travel solutions that have traditionally been on offer for visitors. We have a growing network of partners, including resorts, guesthouses, excursion providers and tour operators, each specializing in their niche within the tourism industry. Whether you choose from our standard packages or prefer something more personalized, our dedicated team will provide a solution to fit your needs, and ensure seamless logistics to enable smooth navigation across our 1,192 islands stretching across 871 kilometers.
Let us know what your ideal Maldivian experience would be, and let Isletot Travel and Tours make your dream holiday a reality!
