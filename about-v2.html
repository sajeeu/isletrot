Diving in Rasdhoo

All inclusive stay for 7 days, 6 night at Rasdhoo with 8 dives

Diving in Rasdhoo, Maldives, offers an extraordinary experience in a tropical paradise with the Rasdhoo Atoll, surrounded by turquoise waters and vibrant coral reefs, known for clear visibility and warm waters. The atoll's appeal lies in its suitability for both novice and experienced divers, providing encounters with diverse marine life, including colorful coral formations and schools of tropical fish. Rasdhoo's underwater landscape, featuring captivating coral gardens, underwater caves, and exciting swim-throughs, adds an adventurous element to the overall diving experience..

Diving in Rasdhoo is highlighted by opportunities to encounter larger marine species like sharks and rays, including grey reef sharks and hammerheads. The atoll's currents attract manta rays and eagle rays, allowing divers to witness these graceful creatures in crystal-clear waters. With numerous dive sites offering unique underwater spectacles, Rasdhoo is distinguished as a premier diving destination in the Maldives, promising an unforgettable adventure for underwater enthusiasts.

Package highlights

Highlights

1. **Marine Species Encounters:** Explore the underwater world of Rasdhoo and have the chance to encounter larger marine species such as sharks and rays, including grey reef sharks and hammerheads.

2. **Manta Rays and Eagle Rays:** Experience the thrill of diving in currents around Rasdhoo that attract manta rays and eagle rays, providing divers with the opportunity to witness these graceful creatures gliding through crystal-clear waters.

3. **Clear Visibility:** Enjoy the clarity of the waters around Rasdhoo Atoll, ensuring excellent visibility and allowing divers to fully appreciate the vibrant marine life and coral formations.

4. **Warm Waters:** Dive into the warm waters of Rasdhoo, making it an ideal location for both novice and experienced divers to explore the underwater wonders comfortably.

5. **Diverse Dive Sites:** Take advantage of numerous dive sites, each offering a unique underwater spectacle, ranging from captivating coral gardens to underwater caves and exciting swim-throughs.

6. **Adventurous Landscape:** Immerse yourself in an adventurous underwater landscape, where Rasdhoo's coral gardens, caves, and swim-throughs add an extra element of excitement to the diving experience.

7. **Premier Diving Destination:** Rasdhoo stands out as a premier diving destination in the Maldives, providing a comprehensive and unforgettable adventure for enthusiasts of underwater exploration.

Price includes

Guided Diving Excursions
The package includes guided diving excursions led by experienced and certified instructors, ensuring a safe and enriching underwater experience for divers of all skill levels.

Rental
Enjoy the convenience of equipment rental included in the package, covering essential diving gear such as wetsuits, masks, fins, and regulators, allowing participants to focus on the adventure without the need for additional rentals.

Accommodation
The diving package encompasses accommodation, providing a comfortable stay in close proximity to Rasdhoo's dive sites. Participants can relax and unwind after a day of exploration, seamlessly integrating their diving adventure with a relaxing stay.

Accommodation

Luxurious 4 or 5-star Guesthouse Stay
The package features accommodation at a prestigious 4 or 5-star guesthouse in Rasdhoo, ensuring participants experience the utmost in comfort and hospitality while being conveniently located near the dive sites. This luxurious guesthouse stay adds an extra layer of indulgence to the overall diving adventure in the Maldives.

Additional Excursions

Snorkeling Safari
Explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life surrounding Rasdhoo with a guided snorkeling safari. This excursion allows participants to witness the underwater beauty from the surface, making it an accessible and enjoyable activity for all skill levels.

Island Discovery Tour
Immerse yourself in the local culture and charm of Rasdhoo with an island discovery tour. Visit key landmarks, interact with the friendly community, and gain insights into the Maldivian way of life, adding a cultural dimension to the overall experience.

Sunset Dolphin Cruise
Embark on a mesmerizing sunset dolphin cruise in the crystal-clear waters around Rasdhoo. Witness pods of playful dolphins as they gracefully swim alongside the boat, creating a magical and memorable experience against the backdrop of the Maldivian sunset.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Rasdhoo
- Welcome to Rasdhoo and check-in to the luxurious 4 or 5-star guesthouse.
- Evening at leisure to relax and enjoy the beachfront surroundings.

Day 2-7: Diving Adventure
- Daily guided diving excursions to explore Rasdhoo's diverse underwater landscapes.
- Each day includes two dives, allowing for a total of 8 dives during the stay.
- Afternoon relaxation or optional snorkeling safari for non-divers.
- Evening island discovery tour to experience the local culture and cuisine.

Day 8: Departure
- Morning at leisure or optional sunrise activity.
- Check-out from the guesthouse and departure from Rasdhoo.

Included in the Package:
- Accommodation in a 4 or 5-star guesthouse.
- 8 guided diving excursions.
- All meals included, offering a taste of local and international cuisine.
- Snorkeling safari and island discovery tour.
- Optional sunset dolphin cruise on one evening.
- Round-trip transfers between Rasdhoo and the airport.

*Note: The itinerary is flexible and subject to weather conditions and guest preferences.*
